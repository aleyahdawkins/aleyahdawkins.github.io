About

I am currently a NSF MPS Ascending Postdoctoral Researcher at Carnegie Mellon.

I received my PhD at George Mason University under the guidance of Geir Agnarsson and Rachel Kirsch. My main interests are in combinatorics and graph theory, especially extremal graph theory and its applications. Outside of research, math outreach is another interest of mine.
